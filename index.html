Lions

Fun Facts About!

  • Lions are the second largest big cats in the world
  • The average male lion weighs around 400 lb, while average femal lions weighs around 290 lbs
  • The heaviest lion on record weighed 826 lbs
  • Lions can reach speeds of up to 50 mph
  • A lions roar can be heard from 5 miles away
  • Male lions are easy to recognize thanks to their distinctive manes. Males with darker manes are more likely to attract female lions (lionesses).
